Virat Kohli

Biography

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988. A former captain in all formats of the game, Kohli retired from the T20I format following India's win at the 2024 T20 World Cup. He's a right-handed batsman and an occasional unorthodox right arm medium bowler. Kohli is regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time and the greatest in the modern era.

To become a good player you need talent. To become a great player, you need an attitude like Kohli.
